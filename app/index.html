WELCOME TO HIGHETT RSL CLUB

Welcome to the Highett RSL Club, conveniently located at 1 Station St Highett (just south of the train station) in the heart of the Highett. Our newly renovated, modern, fully air conditioned club welcomes visitors to enjoy our facilities including our cafe, restaurant, TAB, gaming and club bar.

We welcome our existing and new members, families and visitors to come and experience our new facilities.
